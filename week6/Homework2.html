Calradia

Calradia is a fictional continent and the main setting in the mainline entries of the Mount and Blade series. There are five factions in the original Mount and Blade and six in Mount and Blade: Warband, each with a ruler, a claimant, twenty vassals and a collection of fiefs that make up each faction's region.
The land of Calradia is neither static nor affected only by the player's actions. Each faction is free to wage war, form alliances, or sign peace treaties with other factions.
